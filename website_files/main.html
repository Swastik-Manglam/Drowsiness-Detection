Automobiles have surpassed all other modes of transportation in popularity. As the usage of automobiles becomes more common, so does the number of traffic accidents. When a driver is tired, his focus, judgement, and reaction sensitivity are all compromised. Our main goal is to develop a noninvasive, cost-effective, and efficient sleepiness detection system that can be seamlessly integrated into car driver monitoring systems.
